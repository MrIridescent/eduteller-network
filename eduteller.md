# Eduteller Network

The **Eduteller Network** is a specialized educational advocacy and consultancy platform focused on the Nigerian education sector. It aims to bring transparency, intentionality, and improved standards to schooling by bridging the gap between school branding and the actual quality of care and education provided.

## Core Mission
Founded by **Sholaide** ("Eduteller Herself"), a child-school fit strategist with over 12 years of experience, the network's mission is to:
- **Support Parents**: Guide them in making intentional school choices.
- **Empower Teachers**: Foster professional growth beyond traditional classrooms.
- **Advocate for Students**: Ensure safe, child-friendly, and nurturing learning environments.

## The F.I.T.S Model™
A cornerstone of Eduteller’s consultancy is the **F.I.T.S Model**, designed to ensure a school aligns with a child’s unique personality and needs:
1. **Find the Child**: Understand the child’s unique personality, strengths, and learning needs.
2. **Investigate the School**: Analyze culture and standards beyond reputation or facilities.
3. **Test Alignment**: Map the child’s specific needs to the school’s environment.
4. **Support Transition**: Ensure a smooth adjustment period into the new school.

## Key Philosophies
- **"A good school is not always the right school for your child."**
- **Intentionality over Prestige**: Moving beyond school reputation to prioritize emotional and academic alignment.
- **Digital Transformation**: Shifting from "classroom to cloud" to expand educational impact and teacher income.
- **Transparency**: Providing honest storytelling and insights about the Nigerian educational landscape.

## Community & Engagement
Eduteller builds a community of parents, educators, and school leaders through social media and consultancy, advocating for an environment where children can truly **learn and thrive**.
